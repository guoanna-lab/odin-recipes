Salmon Rice Bowl

Description

Fried rice is an easy dish for a dinner or lunch! It is a filling dish that can be used for meal prep or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup one-day old rice or cold rice
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup sesame oil
  • 1 cup Kimchi
  • Seasoned seaweed pack
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Heat up your pan, ideally you can use a wok or cast iron skillet, on high heat and add oil
  2. Add garlic and green onion and cook them until golden brown.
  3. Add your vegetables or protein if you are using any and cook fully through
  4. Add your eggs and scramble them in the pan
  5. Add your rice and pour soy sauce on top. Toss until rice is crispy and golden.
  6. Season with MSG
